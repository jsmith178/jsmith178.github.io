Bio

Jamie Smith is a Bay Area native, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design at California State University, East Bay. She enjoys packaging design, hand lettering, logo design, and branding. She is also well versed in web design, print design, illustration and drawing. Some of her past work includes menu design, book cover illustration, and sampler kit packaging. She spends much of her free time outdoors, where she continues to draw inspiration from the world around her.
